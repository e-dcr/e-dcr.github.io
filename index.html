Eleonora Di Carluccio


Hello, I'm Eleonora, a Biostatistician from Italy with over four years of experience in clinical research, working with large-scale health datasets and international, interdisciplinary teams. Currently based in Switzerland, I specialize in epidemiological study design, machine learning applications in biomedicine, and statistical modeling of complex clinical data. My work spans from translational research support to scientific writing and reproducible analytics pipelines.
I am currently working at Cardio-CARE in close collaboration with the Hamburg City Health Study. You can also find me on: [ORCiD].
My email address is eleonoradicarluccio@gmail.com.
